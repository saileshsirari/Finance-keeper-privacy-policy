Finance Keeper

Automatic Expense Tracker from Bank SMS

Privacy-focused Android expense tracking for Indian users

Track Expenses Automatically

Manual expense tracking is frustrating because every transaction must be entered manually. Most Indian banks already send SMS alerts for card and UPI transactions.

Finance Keeper reads these transaction messages and automatically detects:

  • Transaction amount
  • Merchant name
  • Expense category

Your expenses are recorded automatically without manual entry.

Key Features

  • Automatic expense tracking from bank SMS
  • Smart merchant detection (Amazon, Swiggy, Flipkart and more)
  • Automatic category grouping
  • Bulk edit transactions quickly
  • Move merchants between categories
  • Fix detection mistakes easily
  • Works completely offline

Privacy First

Finance Keeper is designed with privacy as the top priority.

  • No bank login required
  • No financial data sent to servers
  • All SMS processing happens locally on your device
  • No access to bank accounts

Your financial data stays completely on your phone.

Designed for Indian Bank SMS

Finance Keeper works with transaction alerts from many Indian banks.

Because each bank formats messages differently, the app continuously improves its SMS detection to identify merchants and categorize transactions accurately.

Advanced Expense Management Tools

Since bank SMS formats vary across different Indian banks, automatic detection may not always be perfect. Finance Keeper provides powerful tools to quickly organize and correct expenses in bulk.

  • Bulk edit similar transactions in one go using smart detection
  • Move multiple merchants to a different expense category
  • Merge or rename merchants for better organization
  • Re-categorize multiple expenses at once
  • Quickly fix merchant or category detection mistakes

These tools make it easy to clean up automatically detected transactions and maintain accurate expense tracking without manually editing every entry.
