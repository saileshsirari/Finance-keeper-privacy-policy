Privacy Policy for Finance Keeper

Effective Date: 24 February 2026
Developer: Beautiful Apps S
Contact: androbbmidlet@gmail.com

Finance Keeper ("the App", "we", "us") helps users track expenses by processing financial SMS messages directly on their device. This Privacy Policy explains what information we access, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data.

1. Information We Access

SMS and Transaction Data

  • Permissions: READ_SMS, RECEIVE_SMS
  • The App accesses financial transaction SMS alerts (such as debit, credit, UPI, and bank alerts) to automatically detect and categorize expenses.
  • Relevant message details such as sender, message content, date/time, and transaction amount may be processed.

Access to SMS messages is strictly required for the core functionality of the App, which automatically detects and categorizes financial transactions. SMS content is processed on-device and is not sold, shared, or used for advertising purposes.

Notifications

  • Permission: POST_NOTIFICATIONS (Android 13+)
  • Used only to send alerts and reminders if permission is granted.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

  • We use Firebase Analytics to understand app usage in aggregate form.
  • We use Firebase Crashlytics to detect and fix technical issues.
  • We do not intentionally send SMS message bodies or financial content to analytics services.

Advertising

  • The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob.
  • Ad providers may collect device information such as Advertising ID, IP address, and interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • We do not share SMS content or financial transaction data with advertising partners.
  • Users can manage ad personalization through their Google device settings.

2. How We Use Information

  • To import and categorize financial transactions
  • To generate reports, insights, and summaries
  • To improve app reliability and performance
  • To display advertisements

We do not sell personal information.

3. Data Storage and Retention

  • All primary transaction data is stored locally on your device.
  • Optional backups are saved to a file selected by the user.
  • Data remains until deleted by the user or the app is uninstalled.

4. Data Sharing

We share limited data only with service providers necessary to operate the App:

  • Google Firebase Analytics
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics
  • Google AdMob

We do not share user financial SMS data with third parties for marketing or data brokerage.

5. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards including Android’s permission model, secure storage practices, and minimized data transmission. However, no system is 100% secure.

6. User Rights & Data Deletion

  • You may revoke SMS or notification permissions in Android Settings.
  • You may clear transaction data within the App.
  • You may uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data.
  • You may reset your Advertising ID in Google settings.
  • You may contact us for privacy-related requests.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be reflected by revising the Effective Date above.
