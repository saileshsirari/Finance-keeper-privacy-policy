Data Deletion Policy

Finance Keeper

Overview

Finance Keeper does not create user accounts and does not store personal or financial data on external servers. All financial data is stored locally on your device.

1. Deleting Local App Data

To permanently delete all your financial data, SMS records, and notes:

Uninstall the app from your device.

Once the app is uninstalled, all locally stored data is permanently removed. No backup copy is stored on any server.

2. Analytics & Crash Reporting Data

Finance Keeper uses:

  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

These services may collect anonymous technical information such as:

  • App interactions
  • Crash logs
  • Diagnostics
  • Device or installation identifiers (e.g., Firebase Installation ID)

No SMS content, transaction details, or financial data are transmitted to any server.

Uninstalling the app resets the Firebase Installation ID and stops further data collection from your device.
